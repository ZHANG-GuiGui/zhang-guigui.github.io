BrickPal - DeeCamp 2021

Build something different with you. Say goodbye to stupid manuals and redefine a novel instruction with AR and AI.

User Analysis

After brainstorming, we interviewed 6 LEGO users and then conducted a questionnaire survey on 106 users. We divide the target users into three main groups. They are junior Mocer, novice players, and potential customers. As they have different experiences in assembling bricks, their needs are also different. We extracted three keywords from these groups: personalization, reshaping, and novel.
